Drag-and-drop lesson builder

Assemble SPHE lessons using simple drag-and-drop blocks. Start from proven templates or customise content to suit your class.

  • Drag-and-drop workflow
  • Combine video, discussion, activities, reflection and assessment
  • Save and reuse lesson structures
  • Keep all content secure within the platform
Book a DemoBook a Demo

High-quality, age-appropriate content

Browse lessons by year group and topic. Preview content before you use it. All resources are designed to meet the prescribed teaching hours and learning outcomes of the new SPHE curriculum.

Inspection-ready planning and evidence

Comprehensive planning and assessment documents are built into the platform, supporting WSE, SSE and SPHE inspection requirements.

  • Clear planning structure
  • Assessment and reflection tools
  • Evidence of SPHE delivery across the year

Designed for engagement in the classroom

Students experience clear, focused lessons that encourage participation, debate and learning — supporting emotional literacy, self-awareness and interpersonal skills.

Confidence and visibility for school leadership

Nominated staff can access the platform with unique logins. School leaders can see how SPHE is being delivered across the school, supporting consistency and accountability.
