A Modern Solution

SPHE is no longer a “nice-to-have.” The landscape is changing.
Schools need a modern solution.

Mandatory from 2027

From September 2027, schools must deliver 60 hours of SPHE at Senior Cycle and one hour per week in Transition Year.

Teachers need support

Teachers want to facilitate honest, open discussion but often don't have the time or resources to build lessons from scratch.

Students deserve structure

Students benefit most when learning is structured, age-appropriate and grounded in real-life issues.

How it Works

Get started in minutes, not hours.

Step 01

Log in and choose your course

Select from Transition Year or Senior Cycle courses, with content aligned to the new SPHE curriculum.

Step 02

Build your lesson

Use the drag-and-drop module builder to assemble lessons from video, discussion, activities, reflection and assessment blocks.

Step 03

Deliver and track

Deliver engaging lessons in class and keep track of planning, progress and assessment documentation.
